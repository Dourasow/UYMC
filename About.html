About Us

We are Liberia's best organization

OUR MISSION IS to empower youth to overcome their obstacles and chase their dreams. UYMC is a non profit organization that provides curriculum based programming to reach any youth in any setting with a mission to empower youth to overcome their obstacles and chase their dreams. We want more youth to be passionate about their potential and to build their confidence so they are empowered to chase big dreams. Our programming focuses on teaching youth to learn the importance of finding their passion, building the right team around them, and developing the grit to take on the obstacles in their path in the pursuit of their dreams.
